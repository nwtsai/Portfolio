Introduction

Skills

I've utilized a variety of programming languages in a variety of ways, such as implementing a RESTful API with Google's Golang, developing several iOS apps with Swift, and designing flatpages with JavaScript. I have also developed many personal projects with C++, Java, and Verilog. I've programmed with C, Python, and bash for academic projects and coursework at UCLA. In addition to these programming languages, I am well-versed in markup languages such as HTML and CSS.

In order to efficiently develop, debug, and deploy programs written in these languages, I utilize IDE's such as XCode, Microsoft Visual Studio, and Eclipse. In addition to my experience with both Windows and Mac operating systems, I am also familiar with built-in commands for navigating in the UNIX operating system. In terms of software, I have experience with Salesforce, a cloud-based customer relationship management software, as well as Solidworks, a computer-aided design platform.
