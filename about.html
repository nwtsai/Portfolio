About Me

  • My name is Nathan Tsai, and I am a motivated individual with a passion for programming. I am currently studying Computer Science at UCLA.

  • Discovering passions is my passion. In order to achieve this, I constantly expose myself to both the front end and back end aspects of programming. For example, as a result of constructing and designing this website, I discovered a keen interest in front end web development, which led me to begin my internship at the Daily Bruin. I became a member of Theta Tau, the nation's oldest and largest Professional Engineering Fraternity. I also expanded my horizons and joined Foundations Choreography, where I performed on stage and discovered a passion for hip-hop.
