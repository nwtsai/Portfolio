Welcome

My name is Nathan Tsai, and I am a motivated individual with a passion for programming. I am currently a sophomore studying Computer Science and Engineering at UCLA.

Discovering passions is my passion. In order to do this, I constantly expose myself to both the front end and back end aspects of programming. For example, as a result of constructing and designing this website, I discovered a keen interest in web development.

Aside from programming, I enjoy volunteering, hiking, and playing the piano, as well as getting involved in on-campus clubs and organizations. I became a member of Theta Tau, the nation's oldest and largest Professional Engineering Fraternity. I also expanded my horizons and joined Foundations Choreography, where I performed on stage and discovered a passion for hip-hop dancing.
